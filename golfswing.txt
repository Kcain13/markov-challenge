Quick Ways to Improve Your Swing

Alignment: Make sure your feet, hips, and shoulders are parallel to the target line.

Balance: Keep your weight centered, not leaning too far forward or backward.

Smooth Tempo: Don't rush; a smooth backswing and follow-through are crucial.

Follow Through: Make sure to complete your swing, ending in a balanced position.

Video Analysis: Record your swing to identify mistakes.

Feedback: Use technology or a friend to get immediate feedback.

Drills: Practice specific drills to work on your weaknesses.
